About

How it all came to be.

  • 2009-2015

    Inception

    Started as an out of the box initiative to compete in Capture-The-Flag contests being one of the handful teams from India to participate in International Ethical hacking competitions.

  • March 2011

    Evolution

    Emerged as one of the best teams now present in India competing in Security/CTF competitions

  • July 2015

    Start up

    A small idea convened in a tiny room to provide much needed security training and awareness on the face of a country undergoing digitalisation, under the dilapidated hoods.

  • August 2015

    What we do

    Taking the prevailing security concerns in the country into consideration, the intensity of the need to eliminate the ever-prevalent cyber imperfections is apparent.

  • 2015 - current

    What we offer

    Now on a lookout for bright and talented youngsters and enthusiasts and help them to be adept with the using and developing of cyber weapons of the modern era for ethical purposes.
